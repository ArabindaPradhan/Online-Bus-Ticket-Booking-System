About Us

CityFlow is India’s largest online bus ticketing platform that has transformed bus travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using buses. Founded in 2024, CityFlow is user friendly. By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits, CityFlow has served over 10K customers. CityFlow has a presence with operations across Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, Jagatsinghpur, Baleswar, Kendrapara.
